# Delhi Road and Traffic Safety Notes

Road and traffic interventions in Delhi typically require coordination between the Public Works Department, Municipal Corporation of Delhi, Delhi Traffic Police, and local ward offices.

Relevant checks for road and traffic complaints:
- Confirm road ownership before work orders are issued.
- Identify whether the issue affects pedestrians, school access, bus stops, metro access, or emergency movement.
- For signalized crossings, coordinate with Delhi Traffic Police for signal timing, crossing phase design, signage, and enforcement.
- For potholes or road damage, document photographs, approximate chainage or landmark, drainage conditions, and recurring accident reports.
- Temporary barricading or signage can be used before permanent repair when there is an immediate safety risk.

Planning recommendation pattern:
- Site verification within 7 days for high-risk reports.
- Temporary safety treatment within 14 days when pedestrian or two-wheeler risk is high.
- Permanent resurfacing, signal, or traffic-calming intervention after agency ownership is confirmed.
