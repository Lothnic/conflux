# Delhi Public Lighting and Safety Notes

Public lighting issues may involve Municipal Corporation of Delhi, Public Works Department, Delhi Development Authority, New Delhi Municipal Council, and power distribution companies such as BSES and Tata Power-DDL.

Relevant checks for public lighting and safety complaints:
- Confirm the asset owner and pole identifier where possible.
- Determine whether the dark spot affects women, children, elderly residents, transit users, schools, parks, or pedestrian corridors.
- Check whether the issue is bulb failure, wiring fault, pole damage, theft, power supply, tree obstruction, or missing lighting infrastructure.
- Coordinate safety issues with Delhi Police or local beat officers when complaints mention harassment, crime, or repeated incidents.

Planning recommendation pattern:
- Immediate repair ticket for non-functional lights.
- Night-time safety audit for repeated dark-spot complaints.
- Smart lighting or additional pole installation where coverage is structurally inadequate.
